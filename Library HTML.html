            The Public Library is in quite & serene setting, which makes it an ideal place for the academic pursuits studies.
            Public library and information centre is centrally air-conditioned and well furnished with a seating capacity to

            academic programmes. Education requires a well-stocked library with efficient and effective information retrieval
            system within the reach at convenient time. Having regard to this fact, the Institute has an excellent library

            of volumes of specialized Books, Newspapers, Journals, Dictionaries, Encyclopedia's and bound journals on various
            subjects. The library is housed in a quiet and serene setting which makes it an ideal place for academic pursuits.
            No wonder, the library can proudly lay claims to being one among the best business school libraries. Public Library
            has unique distinction of having more than 42000 volumes of meticulously selected collection of books covering all
            possible aspects of interests in the field of Management, Computer Science, Journalism, Marketing, Finance, Human
            Resources, Economics, Business Strategies, Operation Mgt, Information Technology and Mass Communication.
